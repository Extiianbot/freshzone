ABOUT

The Group 1 Project is an innovative online marketplace that provides customers with easy access to a wide range of fresh fruits, vegetables, and meats. The system is designed with CRUD functionality, allowing users to easily create, read, update, and delete products on the platform. Additionally, the platform integrates a Single Sign-On (SSO) API, ensuring that users can easily and securely sign in to the platform using their existing credentials from other platforms. The marketplace is user-friendly and provides customers with a seamless shopping experience, allowing them to browse and purchase products from the comfort of their homes. With its intuitive design and advanced features, the Group 1 Project online marketplace is poised to revolutionize the way people shop for fresh food online.
